AppVault lets you hide, disable, and remove any app from your Android device — no root required.

Tired of bloatware, carrier apps, or unwanted system apps cluttering your phone? AppVault gives you full control over which apps are visible on your device.

KEY FEATURES

- Hide Apps: Make any app invisible from your app drawer and launcher
- Remove Apps: Uninstall unwanted apps for the current user
- No Root Required: Works via Shizuku with ADB-level permissions
- Restore Anytime: Hidden apps can be made visible again with one tap
- Search & Filter: Quickly find apps by name or package name
- System App Support: View and manage system and pre-installed apps
- Hidden App Filter: See all currently hidden apps at a glance
- Modern Dark UI: Clean, minimal design with dark theme
- Multi-language: Supports 11 languages including English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, Russian, Arabic, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, and Portuguese

HOW IT WORKS

AppVault uses Shizuku to execute ADB-level commands directly from your phone. This means you get powerful app management without rooting your device. Simply install Shizuku from the Play Store, start it once via ADB or Wireless ADB, and you're ready to go.

PERFECT FOR

- Removing carrier bloatware and pre-installed apps
- Hiding SIM toolkit and other unwanted system apps
- Cleaning up your app drawer
- Managing apps on company or family devices
- Digital minimalism — keep only the apps you actually use

SAFE & REVERSIBLE

All changes are reversible. Hidden apps are not deleted — they're simply disabled for your user profile. You can restore any hidden app with a single tap. Removed apps can be restored with a factory reset.

NOTE: Requires Shizuku app (free on Play Store) to function. One-time ADB setup needed.

DEBLOAT YOUR ANDROID — REMOVE PRE-INSTALLED JUNK

AppVault is an Android debloater that lets you remove or disable the most commonly unwanted pre-installed apps without a PC:

- Samsung bloatware: Bixby, Galaxy Store, Samsung Pay, Samsung Internet, Knox Vault, Game Launcher, AR Zone, Bixby Vision, Samsung Health
- Carrier apps: SIM Toolkit, carrier services, operator bloatware (T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon, Sprint apps)
- Facebook & Meta: Facebook App Manager, Facebook Services, pre-installed Facebook, Instagram
- Xiaomi / MIUI: Mi Browser, Mi Video, GetApps, Security app, Mi AI, Xiaomi bloatware
- Huawei: AppGallery, Petal Search, HiCare, Huawei Health, Huawei bloatware
- OPPO / Realme / OnePlus: OPPO Market, HeyTap, Theme Store, OPPO Browser
- Google extras: Google One, Play Movies, Google TV, Google Play Books, Stadia
- Others: LinkedIn (pre-installed), Netflix (pre-installed), TikTok, Snapchat (OEM bundle)

AppVault is inspired by Universal Android Debloater (UAD) and Canta — the most popular Android debloating tools — and brings the same power directly to your phone without needing a PC or ADB from a computer.
